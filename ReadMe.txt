Description: 
This paper has a single video attachment showing the trajectories that were used in an online survey of human preference ratings. By downloading the video, the audience will be able to see examples of trajectories with different ratings and understand the thought process behind the assigned ratings. 

Player Information: 
This video is in mp4 format and will play in the Apple QuickTime Player (version 10.2 or higher).
